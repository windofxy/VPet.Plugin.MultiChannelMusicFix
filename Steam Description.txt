[V1.0.6.1] Multi Channel Music Fix & Microphone Music Capture

功能：
修复使用多声道设备(如使用SteelSeries GG音频软件)时，无法正确检测播放音乐的问题
增加了通过麦克风检测音乐的功能，让桌宠在你唱歌时开始跳舞（需在Mod设置内打开开关）

Features:
Fixed an issue where playing music could not be correctly detected when using multi-channel devices such as SteelSeries GG audio software.
Added the ability to detect music through the microphone. Let the table pet start dancing while you sing (need to turn on the switch in the Mod Settings)
